Spain, a failed democracy

What's going on in Spain?

Catalonia will held a voting on its independency this October 1st. And whast's Spain doing?

Website closing down

The Spanish justice has closed down more that 140 websites only the last week. They were mainly mirrors of the official website for the referendum of independence which can only be accessed from Spain through a proxy, just like it happens in China.

Intimidation to the media

Directors of all Catalan media, newspaper, radio and tv, have received letters addressed personally to them warning them of personal responsibilities if any information on the referendum is published.

Intimidation to Catalan elected politicans

Catalan Mayors are being called to declare in front of the justice for their support to teh referendum. Members of the goverment also were arrested because of their support to the referendum.

Intimidation to civil society and individual poeple

The rights of assembly and expression are being prohibited every day every where the whole country to acts related to the referendum or simply to the right of selfdetermination.
